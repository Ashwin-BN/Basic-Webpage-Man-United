MANCHESTER UNITED

2008 UCL Squad

The 2008 Manchester United squad, managed by Sir Alex Ferguson, achieved great success by winning the UEFA Champions League.

Key players in the squad included Cristiano Ronaldo, Wayne Rooney, Ryan Giggs, and many others. The team displayed exceptional skill and determination throughout the season, culminating in victory in the Champions League final against Chelsea.
This iconic squad is remembered as one of the best in Manchester United's history, achieving a historic double by also winning the Premier League that season.
