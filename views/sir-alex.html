MANCHESTER UNITED

Sir Alex Ferguson

Sir Alexander Chapman Ferguson, CBE (born 31 December 1941) is a Scottish former football manager and player. He managed Manchester United from 1986 to 2013 and is considered one of the greatest managers in football history.
During his tenure, Sir Alex led Manchester United to numerous domestic and international honors, including 13 Premier League titles, 5 FA Cups, and 2 UEFA Champions League titles.
